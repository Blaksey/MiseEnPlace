Meal Prep

Welcome to Mise en Place Meal Prep! Our goal is to provide you with convenient, delicious, and healthy meals. In order to fit your weekly needs, we have several meal plan options to choose from. We have an Exercise & Nutrition Specialist on staff who works closely with our professional chefs to design a weekly menu full of well balanced, wholesome, calorie counted meals. We currently have 1 pick up location in Elizabethtown, Ky.
